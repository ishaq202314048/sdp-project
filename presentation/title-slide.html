TrackTroop

A role‑based troop fitness tracking web app to manage soldiers, record fitness tests (IPFT), assign training plans, and generate analytics & reports.

Core modules

  • Authentication & role routing (Soldier / Clerk / Adjutant)
  • Soldier onboarding & approval workflow
  • Fitness tests + fitness status calculation
  • Fitness plans assignment + progress dashboards
  • Reports, analytics charts, and PDF exports
